Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the calculations?

We use simplified tax rates for estimates. For precise tax planning, consult a CPA. LocumsLab is designed to give you directional accuracy so you can make informed decisions, not replace professional tax advice.

Where does the benchmark data come from?

Salary benchmarks use BLS Occupational Employment & Wage Statistics (May 2024, published April 2025). Malpractice ranges are sourced from Medical Liability Monitor 2025 and MGMA specialty surveys. Retirement contribution limits reflect IRS 2025 figures.

What’s included in the S-Corp Evaluator?

The S-Corp Evaluator compares sole proprietorship vs S-Corporation structures, showing you tax savings, optimal salary/distribution splits, retirement contribution differences, and whether forming an S-Corp makes sense for your income level.

Can I export my scenarios?

Yes! Pro users can export any scenario or comparison as a professional PDF report — perfect for sharing with your spouse, CPA, or financial advisor.

Is this only for CRNAs?

No! LocumsLab works for physicians, CRNAs, NPs, PAs, nurses, and any healthcare professional comparing staff positions vs locums/travel contracts.

Where does the benchmark data come from?

Salary benchmarks use BLS Occupational Employment & Wage Statistics (May 2024, published April 2025). Malpractice ranges are sourced from Medical Liability Monitor 2025 and MGMA surveys. Retirement contribution limits reflect IRS 2025 figures. All data is reviewed annually.
