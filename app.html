How We Calculated This

Self-Employment Tax

For Sole Prop: 15.3% on 92.35% of net income. You pay both employer + employee portions.

Net √ó 92.35% √ó 15.3%
S-Corp Strategy (BLS Safe Harbor)

We use your profession's BLS median salary as your W2 salary. This "safe harbor" approach is defensible to the IRS and maximizes distributions (which save 15.3% SE tax).

W2 = BLS Median (e.g. $206k for CRNA)
Distributions

Remaining profit is distributed. NOT subject to SE tax (15.3% savings!).

Distributions = Net - Salary
Retirement

Sole Prop: 20% of net + $23k
S-Corp: 25% of W2 + $23k

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the calculations?

We use simplified tax rates for estimates. For precise tax planning, consult a CPA. LocumsLab is designed to give you directional accuracy so you can make informed decisions, not replace professional tax advice.

The S-Corp Evaluator compares sole proprietorship vs S-Corporation structures using the BLS safe harbor strategy. It shows you tax savings, optimal salary/distribution splits, retirement contribution differences, and whether forming an S-Corp makes sense for your income level.
